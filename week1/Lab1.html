Nvidia Corporation's stock jumped 8% on Wednesday after CEO Jensen Huang reported "incredible" demand for the company's products. Speaking at the Goldman Sachs Communacopia + Technology Conference, Huang mentioned that “everything is sold out” due to strong customer interest in Nvidia's AI infrastructure. He noted that Nvidia collaborates with every AI company globally, emphasizing that "Everybody's counting on us."

Nvidia's chips are utilized by major cloud providers like Microsoft and Amazon, both of which have indicated plans to increase their investments in AI infrastructure. Huang highlighted the impressive return on investment for Nvidia's clients, revealing that for every dollar spent on Nvidia infrastructure, cloud companies generate $5 in rental income.
